# Audio Files

To add background music and sound effects to your quiz videos:

1. Download free audio files from:
   - **Background Music**: https://pixabay.com/music/ or https://freesound.org/
   - **Sound Effects**: https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/ or https://freesound.org/

2. Add your audio files to this `public` folder:
   - `background-music.mp3` - Upbeat quiz background music
   - `correct-answer.mp3` - Sound for when correct answer is revealed
   - `option-appear.mp3` - Quick whoosh sound for each option

3. Uncomment the Audio line in Composition.tsx to enable background music

## Recommended Audio:
- **Background**: Upbeat, playful music (30-60 seconds loop)
- **Correct Answer**: Success chime or celebration sound
- **Options**: Soft pop or whoosh sound

All audio should be in MP3 format for best compatibility.
